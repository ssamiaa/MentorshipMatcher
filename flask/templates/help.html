Help & How It Works

🧭 Getting Started

This platform is built to connect mentors and learners. To begin:

  • Sign up with your name and email.
  • Once logged in, you’ll land on your Dashboard.

🧠 Adding Skills

On the dashboard, you can add skills you want to learn or teach. These skills help us find matches for you.

🎯 Matches

Based on your skills, we’ll display potential mentors or mentees under the “Your Learning Matches” section.

📚 Creating a Course

If you’re a mentor, you can click “Create a Course” on your dashboard. You’ll fill out:

  • Course title and description
  • Choose free or paid
  • Optionally allow skill exchange
  • Start date

💡 Skill Exchange Option

Some instructors allow skill exchange instead of payment. This means:

  • You can enroll in their course for free if you agree to teach them one of your skills in return.
  • When viewing a course, you’ll see if it’s “Open to Skill Exchange” right below the price.
  • This creates a fair, collaborative learning experience where both sides benefit.

🔍 Browsing & Enrolling

Click on “Browse Courses” in the navigation bar to see all available courses. Click “Enroll” to register.

💳 Enrollment & Payment

Payment info is requested just for demo purposes. Based on your selected method:

  • Visa/MasterCard: Enter card number, expiry, and CVV.
  • Google Pay/Paytm: Enter a UPI or phone number.

📂 Enrolled Courses

After enrolling, you’ll see your courses listed in your dashboard under “Your Enrolled Courses.”

🚪 Logging Out

Use the logout link in the navbar when you're done.
